Who We Are

Vibrant Representation & Student Leadership

The Kerala Students Union (KSU) unit at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology (RIT), Kottayam, has emerged as a vibrant force representing student voices, leadership, and campus development.

With a strong commitment to student welfare, cultural activities, and democratic values, KSU RIT has consistently worked towards creating an active, progressive, and inclusive campus environment. We stand for the rights and aspirations of every student, fostering a culture of leadership, debate, and positive activism.

Historic Milestone

Reclaiming the College Union

In a historic achievement that reshaped campus politics, KSU RIT reclaimed the RIT College Union after more than two decades, securing a massive majority. The victory reflected the trust and support of the student community towards the vision, unity, and dedication of the KSU team. It stands as a symbol of determination, progress, and collective student empowerment.
